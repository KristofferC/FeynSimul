 1  """ 
 2  The physical system package contains classes that represent 
 3  different quantum mechanical systems. 
 4   
 5  A physical system should at least define a B{potential} and the 
 6  degrees of freedom (B{DOF}) in the system. The potential should be a string 
 7  and be given in a format that is understood by OpenCL. This is 
 8  very similar to C however some "native" functions can be used such 
 9  as native_exp. 
10   
11  The variables in the potential are given as xn where n is an integer 
12  representing the particular degree of freedom. An harmonic oscillator 
13  in three dimensions with spring constant 1.0 would thus be given as:  
14   
15  M{0.5f * (x1 * x1 + x2 * x2 + x3 * x3)} 
16   
17  Eventual operators that are defined uses the same way of writing.  
18  """
